Services Details

Enjoy Tea Time as you travel at Sultan Tea Gallery

Serving the tea lovers around the globe at the departure lounge, opposite of immigration 2

Once you are done with the immigration, our team of expertise will guide you to the departure lounge filled with duty free shops where your eyes will meet a fascinating tea gallery known as "Sultan Tea Gallery", opposite of immigration 2 Here you can enjoy some teatime and also purchase Bangladeshi Tea for a wonderful start to your travel journey.

About Sultan Tea

Our tea originates from the Northern part of Bangladesh, offering you the freshness of 100% premium quality leaf. The leaves are carefully hand plucked from our own garden to bring you the taste of Darjeeling tea through bright color, attractive liquor and refreshing flavor, giving you lasting joy, warmth and vitality.

We serve a range of tea to the respective departing passengers including black tea, green tea, orthodox tea and white tea. To further elevate your travel journey, you can also enjoy some refreshing sips of our famous 'Masala Tea', in addition to our flavorful raw tea, milk tea and lemon tea
