Services Details

Meet and assistance

Dhaka’s Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport is one of the busiest airports in South Asia, serving millions of passengers every year. For many travelers, navigating the airport can be a daunting task, especially if it is their first time visiting. Fortunately, the airport offers a meet-and-greet service to help passengers navigate the airport with ease. Upon arrival, passengers are greeted by a representative who will guide them through the airport, helping them with immigration, baggage claim, and customs procedures.  

Price Details:

For General service: 500/- per person

VIP Departure

For departing passengers, the Meet and Greet service provides a seamless and hassle-free check-in process. Passengers are met by a representative who will assist with flight updates, check-in, security clearance, and immigration procedures till the boarding gates. Our team of representatives is happy to assist you with VIP lounge access, duty-free shopping, restaurants, and prayer rooms.

Price Details:

For VIP Departure: 1000/- per person'

Special service for group tours (In and Out)

Our protocol officer will receive the passenger after boarding the Bridge and before immigration, complete the immigration formalities, including the on-arrival Visa, collect the passenger’s luggage complete customs clearance, and accompany the passenger up to the transportation through the CIP lounge.

Price Details:

For group tour service, special discount is offered as following: For 10 passengers flying, service charge is 300/- per person. Please note that this rate may vary based on passenger count.

On-arrival visa assistance

Our protocol officer will receive the passenger after boarding the Bridge and before immigration, complete the immigration formalities, including the on-arrival Visa, collect the passenger’s luggage complete customs clearance, and accompany the passenger up to the transportation through the CIP lounge.

Price Details:

For General service: 1000 /- per person

Special service to children, disabled, and senior citizens

The Meet and Greet service is particularly useful for passengers with mobility issues, families traveling with young children, or those who are unfamiliar with the airport. The service can be customized to meet specific needs or requirements of the passengers, be it with children, disabled, or senior citizens for which additional manpower and support is provided

Price Details:

For special care service: 1000/- per person

Ticket confirmation and reconfirmation

With the integration of 750+ airlines and 600,000 hotels around the world, flyheritage.net provides you with on-stand ticket vouchering from your smartphone and our expert team is delighted to guide you through the booking process.

Internet Service and Printing

Whether you are traveling for business or pleasure, having a consistent Wifi connection might be at the top of your travel necessities during work, browsing social media, or simply connecting with your loved ones. Helpline can provide you with internet access and additionally with printing important documents that you might require during check-in or immigration
